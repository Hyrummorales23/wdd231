Step Into the World of the Maya

Hidden deep within the lush jungles of Guatemala, Tikal National Park stands as one of the most remarkable remnants of the ancient Mayan civilization. Towering pyramids, grand plazas, and intricate carvings tell the story of a powerful empire that thrived for centuries.

Beyond its breathtaking ruins, Tikal is home to a vibrant ecosystem filled with exotic wildlife, from howler monkeys to colorful toucans. Whether you're an adventurer, history enthusiast, or nature lover, Tikal offers an unforgettable journey through time and nature.

Where is Tikal?

Located in the heart of the Guatemalan jungle, Tikal National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, rich in history and biodiversity.

Address: Tikal National Park, Petén, Guatemala
